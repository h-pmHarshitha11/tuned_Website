Experience Overview

The 19th ISTE Karnataka State-Level Student Convention transformed PESCE Mandya into a vibrant hub for innovation. Themed “Empowering Young Minds for Innovative Startup Culture,” the convention blended thought leadership, competitions, and cultural immersion.

From sunrise registrations to a gala closing ceremony, the day featured keynote conversations with ISTE national leaders, high-energy tech battles, student showcases, and peer networking lounges. Dedicated breakout rooms allowed students to explore AI, robotics, sustainable tech, and design thinking with industry mentors.

The organizing committee ensured every touchpoint felt premium—interactive check-in, spotlight award zones, live social walls, and campus tours. Delegates carried home new technical skills, collaboration opportunities, and recognition that fueled their campus chapters.
