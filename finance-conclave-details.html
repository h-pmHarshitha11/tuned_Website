Experience Overview

The conclave decoded personal finance, venture funding, ESG investing, and fintech product building. Attendees sat through narrative-rich sessions that combined spreadsheets with storytelling from leaders who manage crores in capital.

Interactive kiosks let students try robo-advisory dashboards, learn Bloomberg-style shortcuts, and understand sustainable investing metrics.

A curated networking lunch paired aspiring analysts with mentors so the conversations extended beyond the stage and into internships, mentorships, and research projects.

Regional CFOs, equity researchers, startup funders, and alumni from Big Four firms shared tools, frameworks, and hiring cues.
