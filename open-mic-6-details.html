Experience Overview

Open Mic 6.0 transformed the amphitheatre into a sunset festival where raw talent met professional stagecraft. Acoustic duos, classical singers, beatboxers, spoken-word artists, and comedy collectives moved the crowd with original content, nostalgia, and fearless experimentation.

Judges from the music, theatre, and media departments peppered the evening with constructive feedback while the ISTE media team captured behind-the-scenes stories for social channels.

From stage décor crafted with LEDs to on-cue spotlighting, the production felt premium yet heartfelt. The post-event clean-up and reflection circle helped performers exchange tips, swap contacts, and plan collaborations for the next edition.

Quick Highlights

Program Flow

  • Golden hour welcome jam with alumni band
  • Back-to-back solo, duo, and group slots
  • Judge insights with instant audience polls
  • Community finale and gratitude circle

Production Crew

Stage, sound, and light desks were completely student-led, supported by ISTE cultural mentors for smooth transitions and safety checks.

Impact & Takeaways

  • Confidence boost for first-time performers
  • New collaborations for cultural fest
  • Volunteer leadership & logistics credits
