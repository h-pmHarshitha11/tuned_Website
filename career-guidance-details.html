Experience Overview

The session blended practical guidance with real hiring stories. Panelists covered how to position campus projects, translate internships into quantified outcomes, and pitch cross-disciplinary skills for fast-growing roles.

Breakout corners focused on three tracks—core engineering, product & design, and higher studies—ensuring students heard advice tailored to their ambitions.

Volunteers documented every insight into a take-home handbook so attendees could revisit recruiter-approved language while refining their profiles.

Quick Highlights

Hot Topics

  • Resume power verbs & ATS proofing
  • Storytelling for internships and live projects
  • Building a 90-day roadmap after placement
  • Scholarship and higher-study timelines

Mentor Bench

Led by ISTE faculty mentors plus alumni from Deloitte, Infosys, Bosch, and SaaS startups who shared offer-breakdown case studies.

Dedicated HR reps simulated behavioral interview rounds and provided actionable feedback.

Attendee Wins

  • 70% left with a revised resume draft
  • 25 students got follow-up mentoring pairs
  • Career goals board filled with 100+ action items
